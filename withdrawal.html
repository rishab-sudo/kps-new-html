Written Application

Submit a written application to the school office at least one month prior to the intended date of withdrawal. The application must be signed by the parent or legal guardian.

Clearance of Dues

All outstanding fees and dues must be settled before the Transfer Certificate (TC) is processed.

Submission of Documents

Please return the original fee receipt and the student’s school ID card at the time of application.

Principal’s Approval

The Transfer Certificate will be issued only after proper verification and formal approval by the Principal.

Policy Compliance

The school reserves the right to withhold the TC if any part of the withdrawal process remains incomplete.

Note:

To avoid any inconvenience, we recommend parents plan the withdrawal process well in advance for a smooth transition.
