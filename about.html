About Our Founder

The Vision of Shri Krishna Behari Agarwal: A Legacy of Learning

At Krishna Public School, our founder Shri Krishna Behari Agarwal envisioned a place where every child’s potential is unlocked through creative learning and holistic development. Since 2003, his vision has guided us to become a leading institution that combines academic excellence with world-class sports and cultural opportunities. Our commitment remains strong—to nurture confident, compassionate learners ready to thrive in a global world.
